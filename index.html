Selected projects

Research & data work

Sustainable Agriculture

Zero-tillage adoption across 200,000 Indian villages

Analyzed 10m-resolution spatial data to map adoption hotspots and built Random Forest models (R² 0.43–0.65) identifying rainfall variability and soil type as key predictors of climate-resilient land management.

Python scikit-learn geopandas ArcGIS
Remote Sensing

Rice phenology & methane forecasting, Mekong Delta

Used MODIS NDVI time series analysis to generate crop phenology maps across ~80,000 hectares and forecast atmospheric methane emissions tied to flooding and harvest cycles in Vietnam.

R Google Earth Engine QGIS terra
Land Suitability

Land Suitability Index for intervention targeting

Developed a weighted environmental index identifying 34,476 km² of high-suitability, low-adoption areas across 4 Indian states — directly informing subsidy allocation recommendations.

Python numpy matplotlib QGIS
