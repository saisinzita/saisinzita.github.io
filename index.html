About

Science, data, and the land

I am currently a Transit City Expert at Cognizant for Google Maps. My master’s research focused on identifying high-impact areas for sustainable agriculture adoption in India. In the summer of 2024 I interned at IRRI to map crop phenology in the Mekong Delta to quantify methane production. Research projects

Geospatial · ML · Sustainable Ag

Zero-tillage adoption across 200,000 Indian villages

Analyzed high-resolution (10m) spatial data across ~200,000 villages in the Indo-Gangetic Plains to identify climate-driven adoption patterns. Built Random Forest models (R² 0.43–0.65) of meteorological and environmental drivers, then developed a Land Suitability Index identifying 34,476 km² of high-potential, low-adoption areas to guide subsidy targeting.

Field Research · Policy · Food Systems

Natural farming & agrarian distress in Andhra Pradesh

Conducted 21 semi-structured interviews across 11 villages in Kadapa district with APCNF natural farmers and conventional farmers. Applied thematic analysis to understand why farmers transition to Zero Budget Natural Farming, identifying three key themes:

1CSA & community support — peer learning networks and field staff guidance as primary drivers of adoption confidence
2Impact of chemicals — rising input costs and declining crop health pushing farmers toward natural alternatives
3Improved quantity & quality of produce — farmers reporting increased yields and visually healthier crops after transitioning
